# Review Rubric

## A. Mathematical rigor
- Are assumptions explicit?
- Are all formal statements fully quantified?
- Are rates, constants, and norms clear?
- Are proof obligations discharged or honestly deferred?

## B. Numerical / analytical soundness
- Is the computational method stable under the regime studied?
- Are discretization choices specified?
- Are solver tolerances / training details / integration schemes sufficiently documented?
- Are identifiability or ill-posedness issues acknowledged?

## C. Coherence
- Does the introduction promise exactly what the body delivers?
- Do sections feed into each other cleanly?
- Is appendix material properly signposted?

## D. Evidence quality
- Does each major claim have matching evidence?
- Are experiments diagnostic, not just celebratory?
- Are figures readable and claim-bearing?

## E. Acceptance readiness
- Would a strong reviewer be able to trace every major claim to either a proof, derivation, or experiment quickly?
