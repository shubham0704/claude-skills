## Checklist
- Is the claim stated once in canonical form?
- Is the same claim restated differently elsewhere, risking drift?
- Does the experiment actually test the claim rather than a weaker proxy?
- Does the caption state the punchline?
- Can a reviewer find the proof or evidence in under 30 seconds?
