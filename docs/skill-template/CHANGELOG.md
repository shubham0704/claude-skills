follows [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/) and this skill

## Section layout

Each release section has two parts:

- **Promoted rules** — new content that landed in `SKILL.md`. Follow
  the hit-twice rule: promote only after a pattern has been observed
  twice.
- **Candidate observations** — raw learnings from the most recent
  sessions that haven't earned their way into `SKILL.md` yet. These
  sit here until a second incident promotes them (or they age out).
